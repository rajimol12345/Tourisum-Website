Places to Visit in Thenkasi

Nellaiappar Temple

Located about 5 km from Thenkasi, the Nellaiappar Temple in Tirunelveli is one of the most prominent Shiva temples in the region. Dedicated to Lord Shiva (Nellaiappar) and his consort Goddess Parvati (Kanthimathi), this temple is famous for its large, ornate architecture and intricate carvings. The templeâ€™s annual festival, especially during Mahashivaratri, attracts thousands of devotees. The temple's 1000-pillar hall and the massive tower (Rajagopuram) are major architectural highlights.

Shenkottai

Shenkottai is a scenic town located around 10 km from Thenkasi, nestled between the Western Ghats. It is famous for its natural beauty, tea plantations, and pleasant climate. The Agasthiyar Falls is a popular attraction here, offering a beautiful view of the waterfall and the surrounding lush greenery. The town also has several temples, and the peaceful surroundings make it an ideal place for nature lovers and trekking enthusiasts.

Tirukurungudi Temple

The Tirukurungudi Temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu, is located about 14 km from Thenkasi. It is one of the 108 Divya Desams (sacred Vishnu temples) and is famous for its spiritual significance and beautiful architecture. The temple is surrounded by hills, and it offers a tranquil environment for meditation and prayers. Visitors can also enjoy the scenic landscape around the temple, which makes for a peaceful retreat.

Agasthiyar Falls

The Agasthiyar Falls, located near the town of Shenkottai, is one of the most picturesque waterfalls in the Western Ghats. The waterfall is named after the sage Agasthiya, who is believed to have meditated in the area. The falls are a popular spot for trekking and adventure enthusiasts. The lush green surroundings, the serene atmosphere, and the cool waters make it an ideal location for nature lovers and photographers.
