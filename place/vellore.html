Places to Visit in Vellore

Vellore Fort

Vellore Fort is a historic fort located in the heart of the city, known for its impressive architecture and historical significance. The fort, built by the Vijayanagara kings in the 16th century, is a massive structure with high walls, a moat, and a grand entrance. Inside the fort, you'll find several attractions, including the Jalakandeswarar Temple, Muhammadan Mosque, and a small museum showcasing artifacts from the fort's history. The fort is a blend of Indian, Islamic, and European architectural styles.

Sripuram Golden Temple

Located about 8 km from Vellore city, the Sripuram Golden Temple is one of the most famous spiritual destinations in the district. This temple is dedicated to Goddess Lakshmi, and its central structure is covered with 150 kilograms of pure gold. The temple is known for its stunning architecture, peaceful atmosphere, and beautiful spiritual significance. Visitors walk along a well-lit pathway through lush greenery to reach the temple, which adds to the overall experience.

Ratnagiri Murugan Temple

Situated on a hilltop, the Ratnagiri Murugan Temple offers a scenic view of the surrounding area. The temple is dedicated to Lord Murugan and is known for its tranquility and spiritual importance. Visitors need to climb around 1,000 steps to reach the temple, but the view from the top and the peaceful atmosphere make the climb worthwhile. The temple is especially crowded during festivals like Thaipusam and Skanda Shasti.

Amirthi Zoological Park

Located about 25 km from Vellore, Amirthi Zoological Park is a popular spot for nature lovers and families. It is situated amidst the Eastern Ghats and offers a beautiful natural setting with a variety of flora and fauna. The park is home to several species of animals, birds, and reptiles, including spotted deer, monkeys, and snakes. Visitors can also enjoy a trek to the nearby Amirthi Falls, which adds to the charm of the park.
