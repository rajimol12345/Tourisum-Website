Places to Visit in Ooty

Ooty Lake

Ooty Lake is a picturesque, man-made lake located in the heart of the town. It is one of the most popular attractions in Ooty, offering boating options like pedal boats, rowboats, and even motorboats. The surrounding eucalyptus trees and hills create a serene environment, making it a perfect spot for relaxation. Visitors can enjoy a peaceful walk along the lake or explore the nearby gardens.

Botanical Garden

The Ooty Botanical Garden, established in 1847, is a lush, well-maintained garden spread across 55 acres. It is famous for its vast collection of plants, flowers, and trees, including a 20-million-year-old fossil tree. The garden is divided into various sections, such as the Italian Garden, the Conservatory, and the Rose Garden, making it an ideal place for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts. The garden also hosts an annual flower show during the summer.

Doddabetta Peak

Doddabetta Peak is the highest point in Ooty, standing at 2,623 meters (8,578 feet) above sea level. Located about 10 km from the town, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Nilgiri Hills and the surrounding valleys. There is an observatory tower at the peak, which provides even better views. It is a popular spot for trekking, nature walks, and photography, especially during clear weather.

Nilgiri Mountain Railway

The Nilgiri Mountain Railway, also known as the Ooty Toy Train, is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most charming ways to explore Ooty. The train journey takes you through scenic landscapes, rolling hills, and lush tea gardens, with several tunnels and bridges along the way. The toy train runs from Mettupalayam to Ooty, offering a unique, nostalgic experience for visitors. It's one of the best ways to enjoy the natural beauty of the Nilgiris.
